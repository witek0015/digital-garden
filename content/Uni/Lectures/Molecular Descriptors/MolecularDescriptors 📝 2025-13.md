## Lecture notes:
- QSAR correlate within congeneric series of compounds their chemical and biological activity with certain structural properties
- We need to find aim of research, and understand the problem.
- There are over *5000* descriptors, so we need to add knowledge to our data
- **The purpose of using descriptors and chemo informatics**:
	- Design, optimalization
	- Screening multiple compounds for given properties
	- Prediction of properties of unknown compounds
- **Molecular descriptor** is a final result of a logic and mathematical procedure which transforms chemical information encoded within a symbolic representation of molecule into a *useful number* or a result of some standardized experiment
- **Descriptor families**:
	- topological
	- fragments
	- receptor surface
	- structural
	- information-content
	- spatial
	- electronic
	- thermodynamic
	- conformational
	- quantum-mechanical
- **Numerical values** of descriptors are used to quantitively describe physical and chemical information of the molecules
- Descriptors **whim** and **get away** - classes of descriptors so bizzare that even Dr Mikołajczyk does not comprehend how to use them.
