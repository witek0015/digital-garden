# Context
- **Setting in the Multiverse**: The novel takes place in the "Magic: The Gathering" multiverse, particularly focusing on the plane of Dominaria. This setting is crucial as Dominaria is often at the center of many of the game's key conflicts and plots.
- **The Phyrexian Invasion**: The plot of the novel revolves around the Phyrexian invasion of Dominaria. Phyrexia is a dystopian, mechanical plane with a hierarchy and ethos centered around "compleat" perfection in an almost religious, zealot fashion. The invasion itself is a culmination of long-standing tensions and story arcs in the Magic storyline.
- **Characters and Planeswalkers**: The novel features an ensemble of characters from the Magic: The Gathering universe, including powerful mages known as Planeswalkers. Their interactions and conflicts drive much of the narrative, intertwining their personal goals with the larger stakes of the invasion.
- **Themes of Unity and Division**: The novel explores themes of collaboration and conflict. The heroes of Dominaria must unite diverse factions and races to combat the existential threat of the Phyrexians, reflecting broader themes of unity against a common foe.
- **Rich Lore**: _Invasion_, as part of a trilogy, draws heavily on the rich lore of Magic: The Gathering. Familiarity with previous events in the game’s storyline, especially those detailing the buildup to the Phyrexian invasion, enhances understanding and appreciation of the novel’s complexities.
- **Game Mechanics Reflected in Narrative**: If you’re a player of the card game, you’ll notice how elements of the game, like specific spells and abilities, are woven into the storytelling. This can add a layer of enjoyment as you see how game mechanics translate into narrative action.
- **Impact on Future Stories**: The events of _Invasion_ have significant repercussions for the Magic: The Gathering universe, influencing many subsequent narratives and expansions in the game. Understanding this can provide a deeper context for the ongoing evolution of the game's story.
