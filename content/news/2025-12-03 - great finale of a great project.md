The past few weeks have been intense but incredibly rewarding—I had the chance to be part of the **Pomorskie Energy Lab** project, run by Fundacja RC, where I jumped in both as a **mentor and trainer** in the field of renewables.

As part of the **Mobile Energy Lab**, I visited three schools: **Conradinum Technical and Naval School**, **Topolówka (III LO Gdańsk)**, and **First Social High School in Gdynia**, where I led hands-on sessions ranging from **PV panels and electrolysis to mini turbines and basic electronics**. The students were on fire—curious, engaged, and absolutely full of energy. Seriously impressive stuff.

The big finale was the **"Energetic Pomerania 2040" hackathon**, where I mentored the amazing team from **Szkoła Łączności**. They came in strong with **creative ideas and full-on commitment**, and they ended up **winning on the first day** of the event—a moment of huge pride and real joy to witness.

Huge thanks to all the schools, students, and organizers (**Fundacja RC** and **Bartosz Duraj**)—it was a blast working with you. Projects like this genuinely make you believe the future of energy is in good hands.
