After a year-long break from the front lines, I’ve officially returned as **President of the Chemical Business Student Club** at the University of Gdańsk! 🎉

This marks the **third time I’ve held a leadership position** within the club during my five years of studying, and I couldn't be more excited (and honestly, a bit sentimental) to step back into a role that has shaped so much of my academic and professional journey.

The decision wasn’t made lightly — but a few nudges from friends, club members, and most notably **Anita** (my girlfriend and the previous president herself ❤️) reminded me how much I care about this community and its mission. With their encouragement, I threw my name back into the ring — and here we are.

This year, I’m looking forward to taking our activities to the next level: more events, more collaboration with industry, more opportunities for students to explore what “chemical business” really means in practice. Whether it’s organizing conferences like ChemBiŚ, hosting outreach events, or simply creating a space for curious minds to connect — I'm all in.

Here’s to a year full of challenges, growth, and great science. Let’s make it count!

See ya, 
Wiktor
