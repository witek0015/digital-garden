### Interesting fact
**Did you know that bamboo is one of the fastest-growing plants in the world?** Some species of bamboo can grow up to 91 cm (35 in) within a 24-hour period, at a rate of almost 4 cm (1.6 in) an hour! Bamboo’s rapid growth rate is due to a unique rhizome-dependent system, but the growth occurs only during the growing season. This incredible growth rate makes bamboo a highly sustainable resource for building materials and a key element in ecological conservation efforts.
