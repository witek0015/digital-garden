### Interesting fact
**Did you know that the longest word in the English language is 189,819 letters long?** This word is the chemical name for the protein nicknamed "Titin." It's the largest known protein and its full name begins with "Methionyl..." and ends with "...isoleucine." The word is so long that it takes about three and a half hours to pronounce in full! This monstrous term isn't found in dictionaries, primarily because chemical names fall under systematic nomenclature rather than conventional language usage, but it holds the record for the longest word ever used in literature.
