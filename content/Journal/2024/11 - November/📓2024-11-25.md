### Interesting fact
**there are types of galaxies known as "Green Pea" galaxies.** These galaxies were discovered by citizen scientists through the Galaxy Zoo project, an online crowdsourcing astronomy project. Green Pea galaxies are named for their appearance and color in space imagery: small, round, and green. They're actually quite compact and emit strong green light due to their high levels of ionized oxygen. Despite their size, they're incredibly active, forming stars at an extraordinary rate, which helps astronomers study galaxy formation and starburst activities in the early universe.
