### Interesting fact
**some birds are known to hold "funerals" for their dead** Studies have shown that species like crows, ravens, and magpies engage in behaviors that appear to be mourning rituals. When one of these birds dies, others often gather around the deceased, calling loudly. Researchers suggest this might be a way to learn about potential dangers in their environment, as the gathering and vocalizations increase after they observe a dead bird. This behavior indicates a complex level of social awareness and intelligence among these birds.
