### Interesting fact
**Did you know that helium rain could be occurring on Jupiter and Saturn** According to astrophysical models, the extreme pressures and temperatures inside these gas giants can cause helium to separate from hydrogen and form droplets. These droplets then "rain" down toward the planets' cores. This phenomenon not only changes our understanding of the atmospheric composition and internal structure of these planets but also affects their overall evolution and magnetic fields. This fascinating insight into planetary science showcases just how dynamic and complex the environments on other planets can be.
