### Interesting fact
**"Debugging" in programming was popularized by Grace Hopper when she literally found a moth causing trouble in a computer** While working on the Harvard Mark II computer in 1947, Hopper and her team discovered a moth stuck in a relay, which was causing the machine to malfunction. They removed the moth and taped it in their logbook with the notation "First actual case of bug being found." This event is often cited humorously as the origin of the term "debugging" in the computing world.
