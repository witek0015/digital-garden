### Interesting fact
**The concept of "ghost kitchens".** These are essentially commercial cooking facilities operated for the exclusive purpose of delivering food. They don't have dine-in areas or takeout options. Ghost kitchens are a recent innovation in the food service industry, driven by the rise of app-based food delivery services like UberEats, DoorDash, and others. This business model allows for operating multiple restaurant brands and menus from a single location, optimizing the use of space and resources in a market where food delivery is booming. This model is reshaping how restaurants think about real estate and customer engagement in the digital age.
