### Interesting fact
**Did you know that the mantis shrimp has one of the most complex visual systems known to science?** The mantis shrimp possesses 16 types of color receptors, whereas humans have only three. This allows them to see a spectrum far beyond the capacity of the human eye, including ultraviolet light.
