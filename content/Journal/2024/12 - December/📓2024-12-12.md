### Interesting fact
**Did you know about aerogels?** Aerogels are among the lightest solid materials on the planet, made up of up to 99.8% air. They hold incredible records for material properties, including best insulator and lowest density solid. Aerogels are created by replacing the liquid component of a gel with gas in a process called supercritical drying. This leaves behind a highly porous and lightweight structure. Due to their composition, aerogels are excellent thermal insulators and are used in a variety of applications, from insulating spacecraft to making lighter running shoes.
