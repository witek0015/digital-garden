### Interesting fact
**Did you know about the underground "Stone Forest" in China?** Known as the Shilin Stone Forest, this remarkable natural wonder is located in Yunnan Province. The forest covers an area of 400 square kilometers (about 150 square miles) and features tall rocks that resemble petrified trees, creating the illusion of a forest made of stone. These formations, geologically known as karsts, are believed to be over 270 million years old, formed by the dissolution of limestone and shaped by wind and water over millennia. The Stone Forest is also home to some of the only remaining examples of a now extinct species of tree once prevalent throughout the area, adding to its mystique and scientific value.
