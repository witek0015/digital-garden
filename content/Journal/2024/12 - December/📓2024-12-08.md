### Interesting fact
**Did you know that honey never spoils?** Archaeologists have found pots of honey in ancient Egyptian tombs that are over 3000 years old and still perfectly edible. Honey's longevity can be attributed to its unique chemical properties: it is low in moisture and highly acidic, making it a hostile environment for bacteria and microorganisms. Additionally, its natural sugars are hygroscopic, meaning they attract water, which also helps in inhibiting the growth of microbes.
