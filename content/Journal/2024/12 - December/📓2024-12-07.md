### Interesting fact
**Water can boil and freeze at the same time, a phenomenon known as the 'triple point'.** This occurs under specific conditions where the temperature and pressure are just right for the three phases (gas, liquid, and solid) of water to coexist in thermodynamic equilibrium. This can be demonstrated under controlled laboratory conditions and is a striking example of the peculiar behaviors substances can exhibit under various physical constraints.
