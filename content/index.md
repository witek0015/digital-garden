Welcome to my digital greenhouse of thoughts! I’m a digital chemistry student with a mild obsession for learning, tinkering, and overthinking things into new side projects. This space is where I cultivate notes, explore weird ideas, document what I learn, and occasionally pretend I know what I’m doing.

It’s a personal archive of experiments — both scientific and digital — where I plant ideas and see which ones grow (and which ones combust):

- **Projects** – things I’ve built, broken, or abandoned.
- **News** – updates on site changes or random life updates.
- **Media reviews** – games, movies, and the rest of the dopamine diet.
- **Book and article notes** – what I’ve read, and what I actually remember.
- **Notes** – loosely connected ideas and late-night ramblings.
- **Résumé/CV** – sorta like *about me* page, also useful if you’re a future employer 👀
